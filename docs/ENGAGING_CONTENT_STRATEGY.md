# Engaging Content Strategy
**Goal:** Create ranking videos that people actually want to watch

## [ERROR] What's NOT Working (Current Problems)

### Bad Examples from RankRiot:
- "TOP 10 MOST EXTREME DESERT LANDSCAPES ON EARTH RANKED!"
- "UNLOCK 10X HAPPINESS IN 1 MINUTE"
- "UNLEASH 90% HAPPINESS IN 60 SECONDS"

### Why These Fail:
1. **ALL CAPS** - Looks like spam
2. **Fake promises** - "10X happiness in 60 seconds" is obviously false
3. **Generic topics** - "Desert landscapes" has been done 1000 times
4. **No personality** - Could be from any channel
5. **No reason to watch** - Viewer thinks "I've seen this before"

---

## [OK] What WILL Work (New Strategy)

### Trending Topic Research
**Before generating ANY video, ask:**
1. What's trending on YouTube Shorts RIGHT NOW?
2. What ranking topics have I NOT seen recently?
3. What would make ME click and watch?

### Core Principles:
1. **TRENDY** - Tap into current interests, memes, viral moments
2. **SPECIFIC** - Not "beautiful places", but "Hidden Gems in Tokyo"
3. **UNIQUE ANGLE** - What makes THIS ranking different?
4. **PERSONALITY** - Has a voice/style people recognize
5. **SATISFYING** - Visual payoff (ASMR, satisfying moments, transformations)

##  Title Formula (60 chars max)

### [OK] GOOD Formula:
`[Descriptive] [Specific Category] Ranked`

Examples:
- "Most Satisfying Soap Cutting Moments Ranked"
- "Underrated Cozy Cafes in Portland Ranked"
- "Best Lo-Fi Beats for Studying Ranked"

### [ERROR] BAD (Don't Use):
- "TOP 10 MOST EXTREME [GENERIC THING] RANKED!" (spam)
- "You won't believe #1 — " (clickbait)
- "UNLOCK 10X..." (fake promises)

## [DESIGN] Visual Quality Standards

### Must-Haves:
1. **High-quality clips** - No low-res stock footage
2. **Smooth transitions** - Not jarring cuts
3. **Consistent aesthetic** - Each video has a vibe
4. **Visible background** - No more black screens!
5. **Readable subtitles** - 56pt, clear contrast

### Aesthetic Themes:
- **Cozy/Chill:** Warm colors, soft transitions, lo-fi music
- **Energetic:** Bright colors, fast cuts, upbeat music
- **Aesthetic:** Pastel colors, dreamy effects, ambient sounds
- **Satisfying:** Clean visuals, ASMR sounds, smooth loops

## [IDEA] Topic Generator Prompt

When generating topics, ask:
1. Would I actually click this?
2. Does it offer something I haven't seen?
3. Is it specific enough to stand out?
4. Will the visuals be satisfying?
5. Can I finish it wanting to share?

If answer is NO to any question → reject the topic
